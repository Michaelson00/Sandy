A Letter Just for You

Dear Sandra,

Today the world is a little brighter, a little warmer, and a whole lot more joyful, because it's your birthday. Words feel almost too small for everything we want to say, but let us try.

You have this rare, beautiful gift of making every room feel like home the moment you walk in. Your laugh is contagious, your kindness is boundless, and your spirit is one of the most extraordinary things we've ever had the privilege of being close to.

We've watched you face challenges that would have broken most people, and instead you turned them into stepping stones. We've seen you give generously when you had little, love fiercely when it was hard, and smile through moments that deserved tears. That takes a kind of grace that simply cannot be taught.

On this special day, we hope you feel every ounce of the love, admiration, and gratitude that the people around you carry for you, because it is immense. You deserve every good thing this year has to offer, and we're going to be right here cheering you on through all of it.

Here's to you, Sandy, the celebrations, the adventures still ahead, and every beautiful chapter yet to be written. Happy Birthday. 🥂

With all the love in the world,
Eugene & Michaelson
